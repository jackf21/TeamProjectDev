MyStudy

My Study is a project developed by students for students with the goal of allowing the ease of access to useful rescources to use for studying as well as giving adive on how to get into studying well. We know the pressure of having to study for exams, having to crunch coursework to meet a deadline thats closing in and just the general stress of university, thats why we set up this website in order to help in learning the techniques and getting access to useful rescources required to study efficiently.
